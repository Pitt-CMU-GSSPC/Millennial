San Diego, CA

If selected, you and your team of 6-8 graduate students will organize a one-day symposium at the Spring 2022 ACS National Meeting in San Diego, CA (March 20—24). Member responsibilities will include fundraising, marketing, formulating a theme, recruiting speakers, and meeting logistics. This is an excellent opportunity for graduate students to network and gain leadership skills! For more information, check out the ACS GSSPC page, or feel free to email us directly!

To apply:

  • Assemble a group of 6-8 graduate students
  • Select a topic or theme and compose a 1-page theme proposal
    • We highly encourage themes that align with the Spring 2022 ACS National Meeting theme, "Bonding Through Chemistry"
  • Compose a 1-page document addressing the following:
    • What are your goals as the next GSSPC and how do you plan to accomplish them?
    • How do you plan to incorporate and promote diversity in your GSSPC symposium and experience?
  • Provide a list of potential speakers and include 1-2 reasons why they would be appropriate for your selected theme
  • Provide a list of potential sponsors
  • Provide a short (200 word max) biography including research interests and pertinent experience/qualifications for each member of your committee

Please submit all material as a single PDF document to pitt-cmugsspc@pitt.edu by Monday, 09/21/2020.
