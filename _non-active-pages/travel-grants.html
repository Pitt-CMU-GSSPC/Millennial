--- layout: page title: Applications Open for $500 Graduate Student Travel Grants! ---
San Antonio, TX

The Pitt-CMU GSSPC is awarding travel grants* to graduate students planning to attend the Spring 2021 ACS National Meeting in San Antonio, TX (March 21—25). Traditionally underrepresented groups (e.g., women, ethnic minorities, economically disadvantaged individuals) are highly encouraged to apply. For more information, feel free to email us directly!

*Funds contingent on in-person meeting.

To apply, you will need:

  • CV (two page maximum)
  • Personal statement (500 word limit) that addresses:
    • Your research interests
    • Your academic and career goals, and how the Spring 2021 ACS National Meeting can help you achieve them
    • Why you would benefit from the travel grant

Please submit all material as a single PDF document to pitt-cmugsspc@pitt.edu by Monday, 09/21/2020.
